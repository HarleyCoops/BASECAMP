================================================================================
RESEARCH VALUE
================================================================================

Wheeler's 1906 photo-topographic plates are invaluable for BASECAMP because:

1. BASELINE DOCUMENTATION
   - Establishes exactly what the 1906 camp landscape looked like
   - Provides calibrated baseline for glacier measurements
   - Documents ecological conditions of era

2. PRECISE LOCATION REFERENCES
   - Surveyed station coordinates enable repeat photography from same locations
   - Allows quantitative glacier change measurement
   - Supports topographic reconstruction

3. HISTORICAL RECORD
   - Contemporaneous documentation of ACC's first official camp
   - Wheeler's professional surveying validates historical narratives
   - Integrates with broader Dominion Land Survey archive

4. COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS
   - MLP has repeat photographs from 2000s-2020s at same stations
   - Enables 100+ year time-series climate/glacier documentation
   - Contributes to broader glacial change research
