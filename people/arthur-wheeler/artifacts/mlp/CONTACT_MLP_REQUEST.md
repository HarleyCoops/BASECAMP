## Request Summary

We are reconstructing the **1906 Alpine Club of Canada inaugural camp at Yoho Pass, BC** (July 8-16, 1906) for the BASECAMP project. Arthur Oliver Wheeler was the ACC's official surveyor and documented the route and landscape using his photo-topographic method.

We seek access to Wheeler's digitized photo-topographic glass plates from the **Dominion Land Survey**, specifically:
- **Survey period:** 1904-1910
- **Geographic scope:** Yoho Pass, Emerald Lake, Summit Lake, Wapta Icefield area, Burgess Pass, Mount Stephen, Takakkaw Falls
- **Format:** Digitized plates with metadata

---

## Specific Data Requested

For each plate:
1. Station number and date
2. Geographic coordinates/location description
3. Map reference (associated topographic sheet)
4. Digital image URL (high-resolution TIFF or master scan)
5. Associated repeat photograph from MLP field campaigns (if available)

---

## Key Stations of Interest (1906 ACC Camp)

Based on ACC inaugural camp documentation, we need plates from:
- **Yoho Pass** (camp base location)
- **Emerald Lake** (access route and glacial documentation)
- **Wapta Icefield** (glacier conditions, 1906 baseline)
- **Mount Stephen / Field area** (surrounding topography)
