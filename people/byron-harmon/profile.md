# Byron Harmon (1876–1942)

## Role at 1906 Yoho Camp
Graduating/active founding member of the Alpine Club of Canada; **official club photographer**. Attended the inaugural ACC camp at Yoho Pass (Summit Lake) in 1906 and documented the event.

## Bio Hook
American-born photographer who established his photographic business in Banff, Alberta (1903–1942) and became the dominant visual record-keeper of the Canadian Rockies. Founded member of the Alpine Club of Canada in 1906. His fonds of 6,500+ negatives and prints at the Whyte Museum represents the most comprehensive photographic archive of Canadian mountain geography and mountaineering culture during the early 20th century.
