# Reverend George Rex Boyer Kinney (1872-1961)

## Role at 1906 Yoho Camp
Missionary, alpinist, and photographer. Climbed Mt. Stephen during the Alpine Club of Canada's inaugural Yoho Pass camp (1906). Contributed the chapter "Mt. Stephen" to Canadian Alpine Journal Vol. 1, No. 1 (1907) and is credited with multiple photographs in that publication.

## Bio Hook
United Methodist missionary turned mountaineer. Active member of the Alpine Club of Canada from its founding in 1906. Known for controversial claim to first ascent of Mt. Robson (1909) with guide Donald "Curly" Phillips. Produced commercial photographic work from mountaineering expeditions, 1905-1910+. Lived to age 89.
