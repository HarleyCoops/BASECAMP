1906 YOHO CAMP ARTIFACTS LOCATIONS
----------------------------------
All verified materials currently at Whyte Museum (V653):
  - Nitrate negatives (1906-1909)
  - Postcards/lantern slides (1900-1906, hand-colored by Mary Vaux)
  - Prints (1897-1907, glacier and mountain views)
  - Geographic coverage: Emerald Lake, Yoho Pass, Twin Falls, VP Peak,
    Asulkan Glacier, Illecillewaet Glacier

GLACIER MONITORING SIGNIFICANCE
-------------------------------
The Vaux brothers' 1897-1913 photographic sequences established the foundation
for Canadian glacier monitoring through repeat photography methodology. Their
1906 expedition documentation is foundational to understanding glacial recession
in the Canadian Rockies and is used by Parks Canada in heritage programs.
