# Gottfried Feuz

**Birth–Death:** 1872–1949  
**Nationality:** Swiss (Grindelwald, Canton Bern)  
**Role at 1906 Yoho Camp:** Mountain guide for Alpine Club of Canada camp; specific assignments TBD pending archive access

## Bio Hook
Gottfried Feuz (1872–1949) was a Swiss mountain guide who, like his contemporary Édouard Feuz Sr., brought Alpine expertise to the Canadian Rockies as part of the CPR's effort to attract premium mountaineering clients in the early 1900s. He co-led expeditions alongside Édouard Sr. at the 1906 Alpine Club of Canada Yoho Pass camp, contributing to the establishment of Swiss alpine traditions in Canadian mountaineering. Relationship to Édouard Sr. (whether family member or fellow guide) requires clarification through archival research.
