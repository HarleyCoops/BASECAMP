# Mary Morris Vaux Walcott (1860–1940)

**Role at 1906 Yoho camp:** Charter member and founder, Alpine Club of Canada; attended inaugural summer camp at Yoho Pass (July 8–16, 1906); female mountaineer and glacier documentarian.

**Bio hook:** Philadelphia Quaker naturalist, glaciologist, and botanical watercolorist. First white woman to climb Mount Stephen (1900, age 40). Pioneer glacier surveyor with brothers George Jr. and William; conducted annual Illecillewaet Glacier measurements (1898–1922+). Later married Smithsonian Secretary Charles Doolittle Walcott (1914); produced "North American Wild Flowers" (5 vols., 1925–1929).
